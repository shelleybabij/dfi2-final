A selection of paintings that communicate the beauty of our surroundings. They are observations of a momentary glimpse. Lighting, shadows and colour will all be changed in a split second. My aim is to observe, and interpret what is all around me.

Please view my portfolio and feel free to contact me. My work will be showcased with the Brockville Artist's studio in the spring. I am available to do commissioned work; email me directly for a consultation.

My studio is located in Athens, Ontario. A scheduled viewing can be arranged by email.
